    How to Play Basket Random?

    Basket Random is an entertaining and unpredictable basketball game where the objective is to outscore your opponent.     Character Movement

    Move Left and Right:

    Use the A (left) and D (right) keys on the keyboard to move your character across the court.

    Jump:

    Press the W key to make your character jump. Important for blocking shots and shooting the ball.

    Defense

    Blocking Shots:

    Utilise your character's jumping skill to obstruct or intercept the ball when the opponent tries to score. Accurately timing your jump can effectively stop the ball from entering your basket.

    Steal the Ball:

    You can try to take the ball from your opponent. Jump in front of them to intercept the ball when they shoot or move.

    Attack

    Shoot the Ball:

    Press the Spacebar to throw the ball at the hoop. Aim your shot while moving your character.

    Positioning:

    Make sure your character is lined up with the hoop. Move fast to get into a good shooting spot when you have the ball.
